Continue reading the main story Photo Valerie Ruoting Gong and Gregory Earl Ambrosia were married Nov. 19 at Immaculate Heart of Mary in Atlanta. The Rev. Ragan Shriver, a Roman Catholic priest and a friend of the couple, performed the ceremony. The bride, 31, and groom, 34, met at Harvard, where she received a master’s degree in statistics and an M.B.A., and he an M.B.A. Mrs. Ambrosia is a management consultant, in Atlanta, of the Boston Consulting Group. She graduated from Yale. She is the daughter of Min Xiao and Yixing Gong of Atlanta. The bride’s father works in Atlanta as an internal consultant for IBM. Mr. Ambrosia is the chief executive of City Wide Building Services, a commercial building services company in Dallas. He graduated from the United States Military Academy. From 2005 to 2011, Mr. Ambrosia served as an infantry officer in the Army, achieving the rank of captain. He was awarded a Bronze Star and a Silver Star for his actions while serving two deployments in Afghanistan, from 2007-8 and again from 2010-11.
